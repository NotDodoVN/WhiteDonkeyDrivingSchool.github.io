About Us

30 Years Of Experience Behind The Wheel

Learning to drive is an important milestone in anyone’s life, which is why our driving instructors are among the finest and most professional in the country. We are confident that driving lessons with us will be both fun and enjoyable. Our instructors are local to the areas they teach so you can be sure you will get expert driving tuition on the roads and testing areas around where you live.

We know that drivers come in all shapes and sizes, a bit like cars, so your Driving Lessons will be structured to suit YOU. If you’re an absolute beginner: give us a call. If you’re a nervous driver: no problem. We include low risk driving skills in our state of the art facility’s (see below) and we can also do express test preparation lessons. Whatever your age, ability or driving experience we have the perfect driving lessons for you. Get moving with Whitehorse!
